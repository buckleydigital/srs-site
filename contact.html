Send us an email

We read every email and aim to respond within 1–2 business days. For urgent matters relating to a matched installer, please include your postcode and the installer's name in the subject line.

Email Us Now Or write directly to contact@sunreadysolar.com.au

⏱️ Response times

We typically respond within 1–2 business days Monday–Friday. We're a small, independent Australian team — no chatbots or offshore call centres. If your question is time-sensitive (e.g. you're mid-installation and something's wrong), say so in the subject line and we'll prioritise it.
