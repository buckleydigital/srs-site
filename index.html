FAQ

Common questions,
honest answers

Is this really free? What's the catch?
Genuinely free. We may in some cases earn a referral fee from the installer if and when you choose to proceed, but only then, and it doesn't affect your quoted price. The guides, tools, and quote service are completely free with no obligation whatsoever.
How are your installers vetted?
Every installer must hold current CEC accreditation, have verifiable reviews, and carry appropriate insurance. We audit the network regularly and remove underperformers.
How much does solar actually cost in Australia?
A typical 6.6kW system ranges from $4,500 to $7,000 after government rebates (STCs), depending on your state, panel brand, and installer. Our calculator gives you a personalised estimate based on your postcode and usage.
Will I be hassled by sales calls?
No. Installers contact you once to provide a quote. If you're not interested, just say so, and our network guidelines require them to respect that immediately.
What's the best time of year to install solar?
Any time works, but installers are often less busy and more price-competitive in autumn and winter (March to August). Summer brings the longest days but also the longest install queues.
